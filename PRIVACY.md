# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Thank you for using the Airtable Connected Dynamic Form Builder App ("the App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you connect your Airtable account to our application.

---

## 1. Information We Access
When you authorize the App through Airtable OAuth, we may access:
- Your Airtable bases and tables (read/write depending on the permissions you grant)
- Records inside the selected bases
- Metadata such as field names and table structure

We **do not** access:
- Your Airtable login credentials  
- Any Airtable account information not explicitly granted through OAuth

---

## 2. How We Use the Data
The data retrieved from Airtable is used only to:
- Generate dynamic forms
- Save form responses to your Airtable base
- Provide features of the App as intended

We **never sell**, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

---

## 3. Data Storage
- Data is processed only temporarily on the server when needed.
- No Airtable data is permanently stored outside your Airtable base.
- OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only to communicate with Airtable on your behalf.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
This App interacts only with:
- **Airtable API** (for OAuth authorization and record operations)

---

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:
- HTTPS for all network communication  
- Secure handling of API tokens
